Stay Home ; Stay Safe

The COVID-19 Pandemic

2020 or 404 year error has started off with a crisis after a crisis with the biggest crisis being the Corona-virus pandemic. Scientists , Virologists and Biologists are struggling to find a cure or a vaccine. WHO predicts that a vaccine will not be discovered and distributed to the masses before 2021 while some scientists at Harvard believe that Social Distancing measures might have to be in place in till 2022. With most of the countries have confirmed cases and a third of the world's population is under a lockdown , it is necessary that we keep our sanity both mentally and physically. This will ensure that we don't break off from civilization and return to normal life as soon as possible.

How to prepare for the Lockdown

"It is better to be a warrior in a garden than to be a gardener in the War"

It is always better to prepare for the worst , which in this case , is the lock-down. Even if the lockdown has started the following steps can still be carried out.

1. Prepare the Essentials
Stock up on masks , sanitizers , gloves and medicines.
Stock up on at least an 3 weeks of non-perishable food especially canned ones like soup
Water is also critical so stock up on some bottled water of you live in a place which does not have water supply
Remember not to hoard any essential item
2.Stock up on non - essentials
Remember to stock up on times like toilet paper , shampoo , soap
Also stock up on small snacks like chocolate, candy , namkeeen
3. Stay Home even without a lockdown in place
In many parts of the world people were caught out of place , stranded for weeks on end.
Minimize on non-essential travel especially to public places
Don't leave your district/state/country.
4.Prepare Financially
Many bushiness laid off worker or docked their pay. To prepare for this then try to minimize you expense. Stop eating out at restaurants or shopping.
Try saving as much money as possible and don't invest new money as markets around the world are collapsing ! However , don't sell all your stocks as that is detrimental for the economy on the large


What to do during the Lockdown ?

"Opportunities don't happen , you create them"
It is necessary to keep sanity , both medical and physical , during the lockdown.
  • We must stay connected with our friends and family through video call and chat
  • Learn to perform household chores like cleaning , sweeping or mopping , dusting to maintain proper hygiene
  • Pick up on an old hobby. eg Painting , An instrument
  • Learn to cook a new dish
  • Learn a new language
  • Write a diary or a novel
  • Binge Watch a series. eg The Big Bang Theory or F.R.I.E.N.D.S
  • Read a whole series. eg. Percy Jackson or Harry Potter

  • Try a fitness Challenge
